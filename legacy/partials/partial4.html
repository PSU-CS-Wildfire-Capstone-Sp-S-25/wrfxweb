CU Denver Fire Danger Mapping

About

This fire danger forecasting system was built by the Wildland Fire Group at the Department of Mathematical and Statistical Sciences at the University of Colorado Denver.

People

Sher Schranz

Sher Schranz is the PI of the project. She is a NOAA affiliate and also the Associate Director of the Colorado State University Cooperative Institute for Research in the Atmosphere (recipient of the NASA ROSES grant). Sher leads the development of the FX Net platform which will be ultimately used to disseminate the fire spread forecasts.

Jan Mandel

Jan Mandel is a Full Professor at the University of Colorado in Denver and the team leader of the Wildland Fire Group. His main focus in the project is the development of the WRF-SFIRE code and data assimilation of satellite fire observations to guide fire spread forecasting.

Adam Krzysztof Kochanski

Adam K. Kochanski is an Associate Professor in the Department of Atmospheric Sciences at the University of Utah. His main focus in the project is developing and contributing know-how on how to use WRF-SFIRE to forecast fires accurately.

Martin Vejmelka

Martin Vejmelka is a Researcher at the Institute of Computer Science at the Czech Academy of Sciences. His main focus in the project is the development of data assimilation algorithms, recently to assimilate ground fuel moisture observations into a fuel moisture model and to build infrastructure to automatically harness the power of WRF-SFIRE and serve the results over the web.
